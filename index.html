Our Story

Since 1974, Freedom House Recovery Center has been committed to promote, enhance and support recovery for men, women and children affected by mental illness, developmental disabilities and substance abuse by utilizing an evidence-based, comprehensive and person-centered approach. Freedom House Recovery Center combines 40 years of experience with leading-edge best practices to deliver comprehensive and personalized care.Â 
All pertinent Freedom House Recovery Center staff are accredited with these MCOs. Freedom House is accredited with the Commission of Rehabilitation Facilities (CARF), an international, independent, non-profit accreditor of health and human services.
